The Problem

The Challenge of Consistent Dog Training

Most dog owners lack the time, expertise, or consistency needed for effective training. Inconsistent commands and delayed rewards confuse dogs and slow their learning, while professional training remains expensive and inaccessible for many families. This gap leads to behavioral issues that are a leading cause of pet surrender to shelters.

Project in Action

Real-world scenarios showing how PawWise AI transforms dog training

Automated Home Sessions

The device runs training sessions while owners are busy, delivering consistent voice commands and rewarding correct behavior.

Real-Time Posture Detection

Computer vision analyzes the dog's body position to determine if commands like "sit" or "lie down" are correctly executed.

Instant Reward Delivery

When correct behavior is detected, the automated dispenser immediately provides a treat, reinforcing the positive action.

Adaptive Learning Pace

The AI tracks each dog's progress and adjusts training difficulty, ensuring optimal learning speed for every pet.

Growth Path

From Prototype to Platform

1

Build the MVP

Start with a Raspberry Pi, webcam, and servo-controlled dispenser. Train an initial pose detection model on open datasets like Stanford Dogs. Test with volunteer dog owners to collect feedback and real-world training data.

2

Pilot with Local Shelters

Partner with animal shelters to use PawWise AI for pre-adoption training. This provides access to diverse breeds, generates impact data, and creates a compelling social mission story for fundraising.

3

Scale to Consumer Market

Launch a crowdfunding campaign with shelter success stories. Iterate on hardware design for manufacturability. Build a community of beta testers and consider subscription model for advanced AI features.
